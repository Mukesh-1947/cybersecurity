What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks often aim to access, change, or destroy sensitive information; extort money from users; or disrupt normal business processes.

Common Threats

  • Phishing Attacks
  • Malware
  • Ransomware
  • Denial of Service (DoS) Attacks
  • Data Breaches

Best Practices

  • Use strong passwords and change them regularly.
  • Keep your software updated.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails and links.
  • Back up your data regularly.
